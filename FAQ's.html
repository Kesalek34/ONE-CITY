What are the 1City Frequently Asked Questions

The 1City Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) are a list of questions and answers users and developers of 1City use to navigate the 1City solution (as users) and the development roadmap (as developers)


Q : How do I register to use your service?

A: Simply enter our website's URL on your browser, after the page has loaded, click on “Register”. This will provide you with access to our registration portal.

Q: How is my data secured on your site?

A: All customer credentials are encrypted and all logins will be authenticated.

Q: Can I use this service to settle my traffic fine bill?

A: No, our service is for settling your Municipal Account bill.

Q: Why should I use this service instead of e-Tshwane?

A: We offer an option to provide a service to settle your municipal account debt.

Q: Can I use my card to pay for my bill through 1City?

A: Yes, you will be redirected to e-Tshwane to pay using your card. All cash payments to settle your Municipal Account bill are handled by e-Tshwane.

Q: What do I pay for when I provide a service to The City of Tshwane?

A: Municipal Account charges which include Water, Sewerage or wastewater, Electricity, Refuse removal of solid waste, Rates, and taxes.

What features mean the most to me in 1City?

A: The option to pay your Municipal Account bill by providing a service to The City of Tshwane, reporting issues around your neighbourhood to The City of Tshwane, View and managing your account and transaction history.

Q: How can I get help if I have a problem using 1City?

A: You can visit our “Contact Us” page to choose your preferred communication channel to contact us.

Q: What data do you need from me, and why?

A: To register to use 1City we require your identification information to create a profile. Residential information is required to link your profile to your Municipal Account once your profile is set up. Location data will be required from you to verify your location and address.

Q: What if I want someone to do a service for me and my Municipal Account bill gets settled?

A: Upon request to provide a service to settle your Municipal Account bill, the data you provide will be processed and verified to ensure that the service is required. Once approved, all costs to have the service done are paid for by you, and the work done will be inspected and reported once finished.

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: On the Login screen, there is a "Forgot Password" button on the bottom right side. Click on it and you will be redirected to our password reset page to reset your password.
